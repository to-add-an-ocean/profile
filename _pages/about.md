Hello!

I am an MSc student in Astrophysics at the University of Glasgow.

Previously, I received my Bachelor of Science in Astronomy at Nanjing University. During my undergraduate studies, I conducted research in several areas of astronomy, including exoplanets, X-ray binaries, asteroseismology, and Galactic archaeology. Through these projects, I had the opportunity to work with researchers from Nanjing University, Purple Mountain Observatory, Australian National University, and INAF Torino. I also spent four months as a visiting researcher at the Australian National University.
